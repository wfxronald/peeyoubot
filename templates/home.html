Pump It Up Clears Tracker

Be it Advanced, Expert, or The Master title, let me help you count the number of songs you have cleared

peeyoubot

Head to Telegram now and add @peeyoubot to start tracking your clears. Track your grind in a systematic manner!

This project aims to collect the order someone clears songs in a certain folder. Given that a relatively easier chart is likely to be cleared earlier, the data collected by the bot can be used to generate a tier list, with songs that are cleared later by most people classified as hard, and vice versa.

The current progress of the tier list (updated real-time) can be seen here

Bot functionality

  • List down all charts of a certain level, credits to PumpOut
  • Randomise songs of a certain level, or create your own random train
  • Track your cleared songs in order, and find out how far are you from the next title

Check the list of commands in Telegram for a comprehensive list
